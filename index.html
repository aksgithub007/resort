Anandvan Resort

Anandvan is an exclusive resort where you can spend your holidays in peace while enjoying the beauty of nature. At Anandvan you wake up to the twittering of birds, the fragrance of wild flowers, crushed leaves & fresh grass. The rustic simplicity and an unpolluted atmosphere adds to the beauty of the surroundings. The nights give you a sense of wilderness & the clear sky above is truly a heavenly experience. Besides enjoying the peace & natural beauty, one can go into the past for a while as there are many Historical temples, monuments & forts within a few kilometers of Anandvan. Anandvan is located in the Krishna Valley, near Dhom Dam with Sahayadri mountain ranges all around & lush green farms & trees to add to the beauty.

Cattle & Crop

Cattle & Crop

The concept of farm to fork, is about offering our guests a sumptuous and a delicious treat from our freshly grown organic foods perfectly blended with a local culinary art. Anandvan has always cherished its farm activities and hence decided to open our farmstead located in nearby Shendurjarne village,for guests to enjoy and learn farming. 'Cattle n Crop' is opened for all those nature lovers who wish to spend a quality time in a farm. Here we have goats, poultry, cattle and crops like wheat, corn, onion, jowar,sugarcane and many more. Besides,there is a Mango Orchard and Dragon Fruit plantation.

The Holiday Retreat

The Holiday Retreat

A Hilltop overlooking the backwaters at welang, is located 11 kms from Wai at the famous backwaters of dhom dam. It is a 10 acre hill property that offers a complete panoramic view of the backwaters of dhom. Entirely isolated from the city hustle bustle , it is a tranquil location ideally suited for adventure enthusiasts, corporates or a group of family.
